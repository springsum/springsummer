* __occasionally__ oc·ca·sion·al·ly[ əˈ**k**eɪʒnəli ]  : sometime; not often; *부사* ; 1. 가끔     
We _occasionally_ meet for a drink after work. 우리는 가끔 퇴근 후에 한잔 하러 만난다.    
I date _occasionally_, but my work keeps me busy.
